Our Programs

Nursery

For ages 2.5-3.5 years. Our nursery program focuses on sensory development, basic language skills, and social interaction.

LKG

For ages 3.5-4.5 years. Our LKG program introduces foundational academic concepts through play and exploration.

UKG

For ages 4.5-5.5 years. Our UKG program prepares children for primary school with structured learning activities.
